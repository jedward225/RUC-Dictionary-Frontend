1. 流行义｜Trending Sense
自信独立者｜Confident, Independent Person or Attitude
EN: A confident, independent, and unapologetic person or attitude; a “brat” aesthetic representing freedom, authenticity, and rebellion.
ZH: 指一种自信、独立、无拘无束的个体或态度；体现“brat风格”所代表的自由、真实与反叛精神。

2. 现代主流义｜Common Modern Sense
(a) 被宠坏的孩子｜Spoiled or Mischievous Child
EN: (Usually derogatory) A child, especially one who is ill-mannered, annoying, or spoiled.
ZH: （常作贬义）指无礼、顽皮或被宠坏的孩子。

Examples 例句
2021 Just because they aren't behaving like unruly brats in the grocery store...doesn't mean they are scared. 
译文：他们在超市里没有像顽劣小孩那样胡闹，并不意味着他们感到害怕。

(b) 某特定职业家庭的子女｜Child of a Person in a Specific Profession
EN: (By extension) The child of a person in a specific and often mobile profession (e.g. army brat, Hollywood brat).
ZH: （引申义）指特定职业家庭的子女，尤其是流动性较大的职业，如“军人子弟”（army brat）或“明星子女”（Hollywood brat）。

Etymology｜词源
EN: The origin of brat (Modern Common Sense) is uncertain. Nineteenth-century etymologists Wedgwood, E. Müller, and Skeat suggested that it might originally meant a coarse garment or rag. However, editors of the Oxford English Dictionary note that no historical evidence has been found to confirm a semantic transition from “cloth” to “child.”
ZH: brat（现代主流义）词源不确定。十九世纪语言学家 Wedgwood、E. Müller 与Skeat 认为该词义可能源自“衣物”义。但《牛津英语词典》指出，目前尚无确切证据表明该词经历了由“衣物”义向“孩子”义的语义演变。

3. 其他义｜Other Senses
(a) 粗布织物（已废弃）｜Coarse Garment or Rag (Obsolete)
EN: Originally, brat meant a cloth or outer garment, especially one made of coarse or makeshift material; later used for “apron”, “pinafore, ” or contemptuously for “rag. ”
ZH: 最初，brat 指一种粗布制成的外衣或披巾；后来在方言中也用作“围裙、罩衫”，或轻蔑地指“破布”。

Examples｜例句
 1962	I had put on the chequered overall known locally as a “brat.”
译文：我穿上了那件当地人称作“brat”的格子罩衫。
— John Boynton Priestley. Margin Released. A writer's reminiscences and reflections. 1962.

Etymology｜词源
EN. Of Celtic origin. Old English (Northumbrian) bratt was probably borrowed from Old Irish brat(t), meaning “cloth” or “mantle.”
ZH. 源自凯尔特语。古英语（诺森布里亚方言）bratt 可能借自古爱尔兰语 的brat(t)，意为“布料”或“披巾”。 

(b) 一种鱼类｜A Fish 
EN: A fish: the turbot, birt, or bret. 
ZH: 指多宝鱼、鲽鱼或鲆鱼。

(c) 薄煤层（采矿术语）｜Thin Bed of Coal (Mining Term)
EN: A thin bed of coal mixed with pyrites or carbonate of lime. 
ZH: 采矿术语，指含黄铁矿或碳酸钙的薄煤层。

(d) 德式香肠（美国方言）｜German Sausage (U.S. Regional)
EN: Short for bratwurst; a German-style sausage, especially common in the U.S. Midwest.
ZH: bratwurst 的缩略形式，指德式香肠，在美国中西部方言中尤为常见。
