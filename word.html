Semantic Network 语义网络

语义网络说明 / About

原理：基于分布语义思想，通过分析流行语在真实语料中的共现模式，展示其搭配结构与语义邻域。

节点：目标词及其高频搭配词

节点大小：与词频成正比

节点颜色：表示词类（名词、动词、形容词等）

连线：连接目标词与搭配词

距离：反映使用分布的相似程度

数据来源：全球流行语语料库（2024）

Principle: Based on distributional semantics, analyzing co-occurrence patterns to visualize collocational structure and semantic neighborhood.

Nodes: Target word and high-frequency collocates

Node size: Proportional to frequency

Node color: Part of speech (noun, verb, adj, etc.)

Edges: Connect target word with collocates

Distance: Reflects distributional similarity

Data source: Global Trending Words Corpus (2024)

提示：拖拽节点可调整位置，滚轮缩放，拖拽空白区域平移 | Tip: Drag nodes, scroll to zoom, drag background to pan
